Explore the World

Come and explore Japan with us! We at Legacy Hotel offer touring services free of charge for customers spending $750 or more.

Explore on your own and have a great adventure! We have guide books at the ready for you to use at anytime.

Our mission is to make you feel like you're living in a fantasy. What better way to do that than to walk around the beautiful country of Japan. Furthermore we are open to suggestions on how to improve our services involving events and staff behaviour!

"This was one of the best things I've eperienced in my life. Not only do you get the amazing services that Legacy offers, you also get to live out your dream of walking along the cherry blossoms." -Katty Forde, New York Times Editor

Our bartenders are available from 18:30 - 11:00PM for all customers waiting at the skybar to serve you drinks.

It is important to note that we will turn you away if you are not of age or condition to drink. In other cases if you have certain preferences on your drinks please don't hesitate to inform your bartender.
